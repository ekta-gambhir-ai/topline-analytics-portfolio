Topline Analytics Portfolio — User Guides & Resources

Topline Portal
Topline Portal
Topline Portal is customer-facing Topline UI that helps customers download the latest forecast, stay updated with upcoming publishes (includes overlays deadlines), topline announcements and contains topline definations.
Topline Dashboards
Demand Monitoring Dashboard
The Demand Monitoring dashboard helps user track forecast accuracy and demand growth rates for WW countries against different forecasts (OPx/QxG)
Forecast Review Dashboard
The Forecast Review dashboard shows Marketplace/Channel-level growth rates and revisions across key time horizons for the latest (or in review) publish vs the prior and prior guidance publishes.
Traffic & Marketing Dashboard
Traffic & Marketing dashboard provides traffic performance as a contribution to growth bridge for each marketplace/region.
Promotion Actuals & Bridge
Promotions dashboard help customers visualize promotion actuals and promotion demand share among total demand.
Speed Promise & GVS Impact
GVS dashboard provides visibility to daily trends in Glance View Speed (GVS), including the individual GVS buckets.
ASP Bands Dashboard
ASP Bands Dashboard provides a view of the contribution of each ASP band towards demand growth rate (Ordered GMS and Units). It also show trends of levels and YoY Growth rates for ordered GMS, Units by different segments like channel, GL Product Family, ASP Bands.
ASP Rate/Mix Dashboard
ASP rate-mix decomposition dashboard can track how actual rate and mix evolve over time. The rate represents the pass-through of macro spending into Amazon prices.
ASP Bridge Visual Dashboard
ASP Bridge visuals helps decompose the ASP growth into contribution of various factors like Asin rate/mix changes, GL rate/mix changes, new selection impact etc
Topline Reports
TARP
TARP is the central hub for all Topline Analytics tools. Use it to access HVE reports, forecast review reports, and demand monitoring reports in one place.
IN & JP Selection - AWAS Report
AWAS report helps understand the selection contribution to Topline growth. This can also help understand if selection has moved from ret to mfn or medium ASP band into Low ASP band. User guide here : https://quip-amazon.com/HN75AGKnpKFX/AWAS-Report-User-Guide
